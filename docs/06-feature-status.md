## What Works End-to-End Right Now

1. Person fills out the enquiry form on the website
2. Form data is validated and saved to the database
3. The Day-0 email pipeline runs automatically in the cloud (Trigger.dev)
4. Claude AI writes a personalised email
5. Email is sent via Resend with an unsubscribe link at the bottom
6. Lead is enrolled in the campaign sequence (follow-up emails scheduled)
7. Lead is synced to HubSpot CRM
8. Admin can log in to the dashboard and see the lead, email history, and audit trail
9. Admin can mark the lead as booked or add notes
10. Admin can export all leads to CSV
11. If email bounces or spam complaint received, lead status updates automatically
12. Person can click unsubscribe link → removed from all future emails
