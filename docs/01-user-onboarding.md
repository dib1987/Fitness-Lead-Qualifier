# User Onboarding Guide — Fitness Lead Qualifier

**Who is this for?** Anyone who fills out the fitness enquiry form on the website.

---

## What is this system?

When you fill out a form on the Crunch Fitness website, our system automatically sends you a personalised welcome email within a few minutes. Over the next 14 days, you will receive a short series of friendly emails with information about the gym, success stories, and special offers — all written just for you based on what you told us.

You do not need to do anything after submitting the form. Everything is automatic.

---

## Step 1 — Fill Out the Form

1. Go to the fitness enquiry page on the website
2. Enter your details:
   - **Full name** — your first and last name
   - **Email address** — this is where we will send your emails
   - **Phone number** — in case the team wants to call you
   - **Fitness goal** — for example: lose weight, build muscle, sports performance
   - **Preferred gym location** — which Crunch location is nearest to you
   - **Membership type** — the type of membership you are interested in
3. Click **Submit**
4. You will see a message that says: *"Thank you for your enquiry. You will receive a personalised email from us within the next few minutes."*

That is it! You are done.

---

## What Happens After You Submit

Here is what happens behind the scenes (you do not need to do anything):

1. **Your details are saved** securely in our system
2. **Your enquiry is scored** — the system checks how ready you are to join
3. **A personalised email is written for you** — our AI reads your details and writes a welcome email just for you (this takes about 30–60 seconds)
4. **The email is sent** to the address you provided
5. **You are enrolled** in a 14-day follow-up sequence — you will receive 5 more emails over the next two weeks

---

## Emails You Will Receive

| When | What |
|---|---|
| Within 1 minute | Welcome email — personal greeting from Crunch Fitness |
| Day 3 | Club spotlight — what makes your local Crunch special |
| Day 5 | Success story — a member who achieved a goal like yours |
| Day 8 | What to expect — your first session walkthrough |
| Day 11 | Special offer — a friendly nudge with a limited-time deal |
| Day 14 | Final check-in — a warm, no-pressure closing email |

All emails come from **onboarding@resend.dev** (our email service). If you do not see the first email within 5 minutes, check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder.

---

## How to Unsubscribe

Every email has an unsubscribe link at the very bottom. It looks like this:

```
---
To stop receiving emails: https://[website]/unsubscribe/[your-token]
```

Just click that link and you will be removed from all future emails immediately. You will see a confirmation page. No further action needed.

---

## Common Questions

**I submitted the form but did not get an email. What do I do?**
1. Wait 5 minutes — the email can sometimes take a little while
2. Check your Spam or Junk folder
3. Make sure you typed your email address correctly
4. If still nothing, submit the form again with the correct email

**I submitted the form twice by accident. Will I get duplicate emails?**
No. The system is smart enough to detect duplicates. If you already have an active email sequence running, it will not start a second one.

**Can I change my email address after submitting?**
No — please contact the gym directly to update your details.

**Are my details safe?**
Yes. Your information is stored securely and is only used to send you fitness-related emails from Crunch Fitness. We do not share it with anyone else.
